About Me

I'm an elite alpine skiing coach with over 30 years of international experience at the highest levels of competition. My journey has taken me from Olympic Games to World Championships, working with athletes across different countries and cultures. I believe in developing not just great skiers, but well-rounded athletes who understand both the technical and mental aspects of the sport.

Key Achievements

🏅 Olympic Experience

Coached Slovak National Team at the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi, representing Slovakia on the world's biggest stage.

🌍 World Championships

Led the team at the 2013 World Championships in Schladming, Austria, demonstrating leadership at the highest level.

🏆 International Success

Successfully coached in multiple countries, adapting to different skiing cultures and training methods.

📈 Long-term Development

Built and maintained successful programs across different age groups and competition levels.

Professional Experience

Slovak National Team Coach

2018-2023

Led elite athletes in FIS races and European Cup competitions, focusing on developing young talent and maintaining high performance standards.

Québec Provincial Team Coach

2014-2018

Worked in Canada's competitive skiing environment, adapting coaching methods to different cultural and sporting approaches.

Slovak Men's National Team

2003-2014

Led the team through World Cup competitions, European Cup events, World Championships in Schladming, and the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi.

Junior National Team Coach

1998-2003

Developed young athletes through FIS races and European Cup competitions, building the foundation for future elite competitors.

Women's National Team Coach

1994-1998

Coached the women's team in World Cup and European Cup competitions, managing both technical training and equipment.
